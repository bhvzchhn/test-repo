About Me:

I am a post-doctoral fellow at Univeristy of Iowa interested in astro-particle physics. I am interested in meutrino physics that is relevant for current and future experiments. Recently, I have worked on developing the science case for a deuterated liquid scintillator detector that can be indigenously built with resources and technology available in India.
